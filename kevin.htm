Kevin Davis

(this page written by beppo)
Kevin was one of the first members of TTC. He was pulling on the cups before anyone else with Brian "Fro no mo" Magnesun. He has undoubtedly ridden the cups more times than any one else in the team, and extremely likely more than anyone else in the world! While he was pulling with the team he was the #1 top puller in the world. Next to me he holds the most records I know exist. He quite likely has gone the fastest of anyone in the world, but we haven't record anything faster than the .57 Record1.run. of 18 APR 97. The fastest run made ,to the best of my knowledge, was a .50 made by me, Less, Jenni, and a friend of Jenni timed with a class 1 timing method.
