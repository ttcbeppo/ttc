Ripping open

Ripping open hurts. Don't do it. See The definition page for the definition of ripping open.

We don't have much research into ripping open, so there isn't much I can say here. A method I use is to pull with my fingertips instead of my palm. Still grasping the handle in a normal fashion, except pulling up to apply the pressure to your fingertips. No one has ever ripped open their fingertips yet. This makes it very difficult to put in a lot of force though, so we only use this technique when our hands start hurting. That, however, could be after 5 seconds of pulling on our warm up run (the first run of the night to get us used to the extreme environment experienced during a run). If we feel our hands ripping apart we'll usually stop. But if everyone else in the cup is dead (meaning they have no more energy left) then we might try to engineer a method to pull. This sometimes will put us out for like a week though. But it's all in the name of science :D
