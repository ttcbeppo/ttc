Foods of TTC

What we eat plays a very important part of going fast. The food that we eat must give us enough energy to last not only for all of our runs through a night, but also give us the energy to last through any other activities we happen to partake in while at Disneyland. All of the foods which we regularly eat must be high energy foods. Not only do the foods we eat give us quite a bit of energy but also all are quite tasty. Here is a small list of some of the places we like to get things to eat or drink and reasons why we as a team like these places.

Jet Pizza

Located in Costa Mesa CA
(no longer owned by the same people and no where near as good as it used to be)

One of our favorite places to eat before leaving to make runs is Jet Pizza. The pizza made here is the best in all of California, probably all of the North American continent (I'm sure there is a better pizza place in Sicily but they wont deliver to California in less than 30 min.) The pizza gives us optimum energy to pull and will usually keep us pumped enough to pull quite a few runs throughout the night. Jet also helps to stabilize our stomachs. Throwing up on a run, while it hasn't yet happened, would not be a good thing.

Cappuccino from 7-11

Located...anywhere

Running around D-Land going on rides, not to mention pulling on the cups, can really wear you out. It's very important to have not just enormous amounts of caffeine but lots of sugar. And 7-11 cappuccino is loaded with both. It's cheap and on the way.

Carls Jr.

Located all over the western US

Our primary food source, Carls Jr. may not be available in your neck of the woods, but if they are you know what we mean. If not, when in California you must hit em' up. Anything on the menu is fine, but a Double Western Bacon Cheeseburger will keep ya going for many hours. Not to mention it's the absolute most delicious hamburger on the planet.

Carnation Gardens ice cream

Located in Disneyland near The Center

Most people think if you pay for something in Disneyland, it'll be terribly expensive. But that is not always the case. Carnation Gardens proves this. Their ice cream is, belive it or not, a very good deal in or out of D-land. In my opinion it is bigger per scoop and better tasting than Baskin Robins ice cream and really close to the same price, if not less expensive, I'm not sure. Many times me and Brian would meet in the gardens after work and just hang out and eat the tasty ice cream for an hour or two, perhaps make a run, and then go home. That, as far as I am concerned, was alone worth the value of my pass. I am also helping out D-land.

Bengal Bar-B-cue

Located in Disneyland in Advantureland across from Indi

The greatest skewers in the world. A bit pricey, they are well worth it. Also very good is the iced cappuccino. Also a bit pricey, they are also worth the higher price. They are about double than for one of the same size from a coffee shop and triple that of 7-11s cappuccino. While you can put ice in it, 7-11 doesn't have the whipped cream, cinnamon, and stuff.
